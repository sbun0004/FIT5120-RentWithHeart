Rental Guide

Beginners guide to renting in Victoria

1. Have your identification ready

This can be your license, passport, birth certificate, Medicare card, or your Visa letter if you are from any other country.

2. Have your references ready

This could be from your employer. Your previous house owner or someone whom you trust and who can refer you

3. Have your rental history of two years and give the contact of your previous house owners or landlords

4. Have your proof of income, your employment letter or your payslips of the past six months

5. Have your employment details and history

6. Have the copy of your bank statements

7. Have your recommendation letter from charities
