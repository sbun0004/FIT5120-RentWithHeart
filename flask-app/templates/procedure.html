Rental Guide

Guidance on how to do Rental in Victoria
1

Have your identification ready. This can be your license, passport, birth certificate, Medicare card, or your Visa letter if you are from any other country

2

Have your references ready. This could be from your employer. Your previous house owner or someone whom you trust and who can refer you

3

Have your rental history of two years and give the contact of your previous house owners or landlords

4

Proof of income, your employment letter or your payslips of the past six months

5

Employment details and history

6

Copy of your bank statements

7

Recommendation letter from charities
